    About Us

    Dr. Patil Medical Centre is dedicated to safeguarding health and promoting well-being through comprehensive medical care and preventive programs.

    We emphasize the protection of health at work, fostering wellness, and enhancing individual capacity, while proactively preventing occupational diseases and accidents.

    • Our services encompass health surveillance, emergency preparedness, and first aid, ensuring readiness and responsive care in critical situations.
    • We regularly monitor work environments, conduct risk assessments, and implement preventive measures to maintain safe conditions.
    • Through health education and targeted promotion, we empower individuals to make informed choices for a healthier future.

    At Dr. Patil Medical Centre, your health is our commitment.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our services and approach to healthcare.

    What services does Dr. Patil Medical Centre provide?

    Dr. Patil Medical Centre offers a wide range of services including health surveillance, first aid, emergency preparedness, work environment surveillance, risk assessments, and personalized wellness programs. We focus on promoting overall well-being and preventing occupational diseases and accidents.

    How does Dr. Patil Medical Centre personalize wellness programs?

    We design wellness programs based on individual health metrics such as BMI, cholesterol levels, blood sugar, and blood pressure. Each program is tailored to address specific health needs and goals, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.

    Do you offer emergency medical services?

    Yes, we provide immediate first aid and emergency medical services to address workplace injuries and health emergencies. Our trained medical team is always ready to respond quickly and effectively.

    How often do you conduct health surveillance?

    Health surveillance is conducted regularly to monitor the well-being of employees. We track key health indicators and adjust wellness programs as necessary to ensure the continued health and safety of all individuals.

    What is your approach to workplace safety?

    We conduct regular surveillance of the work environment to identify potential hazards and risks. Additionally, we perform risk assessments and implement preventive and control measures to create a safe and healthy workplace for everyone.

    Can Dr. Patil Medical Centre help with health education?

    Yes, we offer health education and promotion services to empower individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed health decisions. Our programs cover a wide range of topics, from nutrition to stress management and beyond.
